Privacy Policy for Sangana Agrisol

Sangana Agrisol respects your privacy. This mobile application is designed to work offline in remote areas and only syncs data to the server when internet connectivity is available.

We do not collect personal or sensitive user data unless explicitly provided for registration or identification purposes. All captured data, such as stock, activity logs, harvests, and sales, is used solely for farm management and is stored securely.

Users have full control over when data is uploaded. We do not share or sell any user data to third parties. For questions or concerns, contact us at support@sangana.africa.
